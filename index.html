Security Engineer with 3+ years of hands-on experience securing cloud-native infrastructure, Kubernetes clusters, and enterprise applications across multi-cloud environments. I design and operate end-to-end security postures that span AWS, Azure, and GCP — weaving together CNAPP, CSPM, CWPP, and AppSec frameworks into a coherent, compliance-driven strategy.
